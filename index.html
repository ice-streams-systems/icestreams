ABOUT

// ORIGIN

Ice Streams Systems is a Michigan-based firm built on the principle that the people who understand a system are the ones who should build it.

ISS draws on direct field experience across manufacturing engineering and systems administration: CNC machining, die cast production, industrial electrical panel diagnosis and repair, control system design, and defense-grade network infrastructure built to federal compliance standards. The work has been done under real constraints, in real environments, without a safety net.

Discretion is a working discipline here, not a policy statement. Sensitive engagements handled accordingly.

// PHILOSOPHY

Most problems aren't technical. They're organizational. Systems fail because nobody understood the whole picture before they started building.

We understand the whole picture first. Then we build: deliberately, cleanly, without loose ends.

Direct communication. Exact deliverables. No scope creep, no upsell, no vague timelines. Work gets done or we talk about why.

The goal is not to stay small. ISS is built to grow into a firm that designs and ships its own products: embedded systems, operational dashboards, and platform software for industries that have been underserved by technology for too long.
